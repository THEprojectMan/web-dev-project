Textile Designer

Reference Number: job01

Location: Melbourne, Australia

Salary Range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year (depending on experience)

Job Description:

Ceylon Textile Suppliers is seeking an experienced textile designer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating and developing designs for our range of bedding, curtains, upholstery, and other textiles. They will work closely with the design team and other departments to ensure our products are of the highest quality and meet the needs of our customers.

Key Responibilities:

  1. Researching and developing new design concepts for our textile products
  2. Creating and producing designs using digital design tools and traditional techniques
  3. Collaborating with the design team and other departments to ensure design concepts are aligned with customer needs, production capabilities, and cost requirements
  4. Presenting designs and concepts to internal and external stakeholders, including customers and suppliers
  5. Conducting market research to stay up-to-date with industry trends and competitive products
  6. Adhering to ethical and sustainable manufacturing practices

Required Skills:

Essential:

  1. Minimum 3 years of experience in textile design or a related field
  2. Strong portfolio of textile designs that demonstrate creativity, versatility, and technical skill
  3. Proficiency in digital design tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign
  4. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to collaborate with cross-functional teams
  5. Understanding of manufacturing processes and materials, and ability to work within technical constraints and requirements
  6. Commitment to ethical and sustainable manufacturing practices

Preffered:

  • A tertiary qualification in textile designing or a related field
  • Experience in textile industry

Digital Marketing Specialist

Reference Number: job02

Location: Melbourne, Australia

Salary Range: $70,000 - $90,000 per year (depending on experience)

Job Description:

Ceylon Textile Suppliers is seeking a highly motivated Digital Marketing Specialist to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for developing and implementing effective digital marketing strategies to drive brand awareness, lead generation, and revenue growth across various online channels.

Key Responibilities:

  1. Developing and executing digital marketing campaigns across various channels such as email, social media, search engines, and display advertising
  2. Conducting market research and data analysis to identify target audiences and optimize campaign performance
  3. Collaborating with cross-functional teams to create and implement content marketing initiatives, including blog posts, whitepapers, and social media content
  4. Developing and implementing SEO and SEM strategies to improve website traffic and search engine rankings
  5. Tracking and reporting on key performance metrics to monitor campaign effectiveness and ROI
  6. Staying up-to-date with emerging trends and best practices in digital marketing and e-commerce

Required Skills:

Essential:

  1. Proven track record of developing and executing successful digital marketing campaigns that drove revenue growth
  2. Strong understanding of digital marketing channels and tools such as email marketing platforms, social media management tools, Google Analytics, and SEO/SEM tools
  3. Excellent analytical skills and ability to use data to inform and optimize marketing strategies
  4. Strong project management skills and ability to manage multiple campaigns simultaneously
  5. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to collaborate with cross-functional teams

Preffered:

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in digital marketing or a related field
  • Experience in textile industry

Note: Salary range is dependent on experience and may be negotiable. This is a full-time position with standard benefits such as healthcare, retirement plans, and paid vacation.
